SpeakPulse

is a blended solution that provides users their score, recommendations and analytics so they can succeed during interviews and in the workforce. SpeakPulse began with a recorder and a spreadsheet. The goal was to help people sound better during interviews.

Today, SpeakPulse is a full loop software that helps students and job seekers get ahead - through strong communication skills, with higher confidence, and without fear of face-to-face interaction.

Everyone has a different speaking level - and whether they like to admit it or not, everyone can be a better speaker. Because speaking involves many elements, it can be time-consuming and expensive to train on your own. Our promise is that we'll help you find out where you stand so you can improve from there.
